ABOUT

WE ARE A DKU INNOVATION INCUBATOR(DII) PROJECT, AIMING IN HELPING THE ELDERLY CATCH UP WITH THE FAST-PACE DIGITAL WORLD.

WE COMBINE OFFLINE LECTURES AND ONLINE VIDEOS TOGETHER TO OFFER THE ELDERLY TUTORIAL OF SMART DEVICE.

IT IS OUR RESPONSIBILITY!

TEAM

ALL OF OUR TEAM MEMBERS ARE FROM DUKEKUNSHAN UNIVERSITY, MAJORING IN DIFFERENT MAJORS LIKE DATA SCIENCE, BIOLOGY AND CHEMISTRY. DIVERSITY IN MAJORS AND PASSION FOR HELPING THE ELDERLY MAKE US DEVELOP THE PROJECT CONTINUOUSLY.

KUNSHAN

WE STARTED OUR FIRST FIELD RESEARCH IN A NURSING HOME BESIDE OUR SCHOOL. THEN, WE ESTABLISHED CONNECTIONS WITH SEVERAL DAY-CARE COMMUNITY CENTERS. IT MAKES US FEEL THE ELDERLY'S INTEREST IN SMARTPHONE.

SHANGHAI

AFTER PRODUCING SEVERAL ONLINE VIDEOS, WE STARTED TO HOLD OFFLINE SEMINARS IN SOME ELDERLY COMMUNITY IN SHANGHAI TO PROMOTE OUR PROJECT AND COLLECT FEEDBACKS FROM THE ELDERLY.
